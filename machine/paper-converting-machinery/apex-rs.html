Control Panel

  • High-tech electronic control panel suitable for operating all electrical and electronic function

Pile Delivery Unit

  • Blowing system is provided for better stacking
  • Three side jogging arrangement
  • Pneumatic vibrators are provided for proper jogging

Paper conveyor Unit

  • A paper jamming sensor has been installed to halt the operation of the machine.
  • Rejection gate to remove the sheets manually controlled by operator
  • Servo-based automatic vacuum unit to overlap sheets properly for smooth passing

Digital Synchro Rotary Cutting Unit

  • Servo-driven heavy-duty Synchro cross-cutting system
  • Required sheet length is done easily by entering inputs into HMI
  • Lever operated space provision given for easy changing of knives and for maintenance

Slitting Unit

  • Top and Bottom slitting knife pneumatically operated
  • Easy manual adjustment of slitting knives through rack and pinion arrangement

Decurling Unit

  • Auto de-curling facility for paper and board
  • Unit is operated through geared motor
  • Equipped with anti-static bars for each web

Reel Unwind Stand

  • Heavy duty structure with hydraulic power pack for reel lifting
  • Axial movement of the arms is on LM guide with the help of geared motors
  • Both sides Electro Pneumatic tension control system
  • Manual track and trolley system for movement of reel
