Cut Size Ream Wrapping Machine

  • CRM 20

CRM 20 is designed for high-quality ream wrapping with fine paper. This machine is suitable for wrapping A4 Size. It is heavy-duty, automated, and comprises an infeed conveyor, wrapper roll unwinder unit, wrapper pulling and cutting unit, wrapper transport system, central wrapping unit, and outfeed roller conveyor.
