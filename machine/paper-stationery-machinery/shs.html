Automatic Reel to Sheet Ruling/Flexo Printing Machine

  • SHS 91 S | SHS 104 S | SHS 120 S

The Automatic reel-to-sheet Ruling/Flexo Printing Machine consists of a shaft-based motorized reel stand with a digital web guide, a 2-color printing tower, a pile delivery station with a motorized vibrating jogger, and a control panel with an HMI. It is suitable for rule or flexo printing on paper in 2 colors. The machine delivers ruled/flexo printed sheets in cut length as per the requirement directly from the paper reel.
