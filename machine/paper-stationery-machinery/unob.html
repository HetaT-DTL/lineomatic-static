Exercise Book Binding Machine

  • Uno B104

The Exercise Book Binding Machine is for binding soft-title-cover staple pin-type exercise notebooks. It consists of a manual bunch feeding table, wire stitching unit, folding unit, mechanical square back pressing unit, book front cutting unit, book separating cutting unit, book delivery table, waste removal trolley, lubrication system, and centralized control panel (PLC).
