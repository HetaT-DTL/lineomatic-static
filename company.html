WHO WE ARE

Message from the Founder

Thank you for making Lineomatic a respected name in the industry. Today, we are one of the world’s leading manufacturers of Stationery, Converting, Packaging, and Printing Machinery. We are dedicated to helping our customers produce high-quality products in a safe, efficient, and sustainable way.

 

OUR PURPOSE:
  • To drive innovation, we have the right solutions to meet the needs of our global customers
  • To act responsibly - lower environmental impact and work with the highest standards of ethics
  • To attract and develop high-quality talent, we produce cutting-edge, quality products and deliver the best service to our customers. 

As always, we are keen to listen to our customers and partners, understand their current needs, and anticipate future trends. We continue investing in R&D (IoT and smart monitoring), supply chain infrastructure (vertical integration), quality systems, and post-sales experience to ensure our customers stay ahead. Thank you, again, for trusting in our products and services over the last 50 years. We look forward to another 50 years of driving excellence and purpose for our customers.

 

We care deeply about ideas, designs, people, and processes—they all connect ultimately, and the quality of these connections determines the quality of our machinery.

Uday Patel

HOW WE DO IT

Our Recipe for Excellence

Our People

Talent is our most precious resource. People open to possibilities and striving for excellence push limits to help us innovate in products, processes, and customer services. We foster an environment that promotes equality and freedom of expression, and this has helped us respond quickly to customer needs and deliver on our value proposition.

Operational Excellence

Beyond being ISO 9001:2015 & 45001:2018 certified, we live and breathe the values of advanced quality management systems. We have stringent quality controls, from the procurement of raw materials to manufacturing and testing to the setting of final products. We have a 200,000-square-foot manufacturing facility in Ahmedabad with best-in-class equipment and manufacturing systems. We have also integrated backward-producing mission-critical parts and sub-systems in-house that align with international standards.

Innovation

Along with innovative, incremental improvements, our R&D team is focused on breakthrough innovation to create the machinery systems of tomorrow. We’re leveraging IoT to make our machines smarter. Our machines are equipped with advanced preventive maintenance software to diagnose and predict issues in real time. The software also provides comprehensive data and analytics to support your continuous improvement efforts. Our eCommerce shop (shop.lineomatic.com) now makes it easy to order genuine parts.

Global Reach

We have 4000+ successful installations in 90+ countries around the globe. We understand international standards and the requirements of specific countries. Accordingly, we have adapted our product solutions and on-the-ground support to meet customer needs.
